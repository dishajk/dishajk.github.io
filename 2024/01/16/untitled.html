I was at Nagpur this month. It is my first time visiting Nagpur. I was expecting RSS, Oranges and Haldirams. I did have a lot of oranges, brought back some for my friends along with a box of orange barfi. I did not have the time for Haldirams. My friend, S who was traveling with me said that there’s no point in going to Haldirams, one gets to buy them in Bangalore too. S has a point.
