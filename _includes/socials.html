Quick Guide to my Social Spaces

Here's a quick guide on how to find me on different social media platforms and when to use each one.

Facebook

I'm not very active on Facebook, and it's mainly for my personal use. I have reserved it for close friends and family. While I might use Messenger occasionally, I prefer not to mix personal and professional matters here.

Instagram

I have two Instagram accounts. One is all about my culinary adventures - the delicious food I cook. The second is a more personal space with snaps of people, places, and things that catch my eye. If you stumble upon me here and want to discuss work, feel free to drop me a message. However, I'd prefer to continue the conversation via email.

Twitter

Twitter is my place for random thoughts, musings, shit-posting, and the occasional "shouting into the void" moment. If you enjoy a good laugh or some lighthearted banter, this is the spot. Serious inquiries or work-related discussions are better suited for email.

Response Time

Even though I do spend a considerable amount of time on social media, it's not on a regular basis. If you reach out to me, please be patient - I may not respond within a day or even a week. If your inquiry requires a prompt reply, sending me an email would be the best option.

Phone Calls

I'm not the best at keeping my phone charged, so calling might not be the quickest way to get in touch. If you must call, drop me a message about an hour or day beforehand to ensure I'm ready to pick up.

All that being said, I'm always thrilled to connect, whether it's about work or to say hi.
