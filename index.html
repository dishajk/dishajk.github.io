Hi, I am Disha. I studied Physics and Mathematics at the National Institute of Science Education and Research, Bhubaneswar, graduated in June 2019. I plan to pursue a career in research and am therefore applying for PhD positions right now. If you are interested in knowing what I did in the past years, check here.

Over the years, I have learned that aimlessly browsing the internet is an easy but mostly unproductive way to procrastinate. I have found a few alternatives. When I am not drafting graduate school applications, I make TikZ graphics for my friends. If you want any made, you can mail me. I will also be adding it to my 'TikZ-bank' in GitHub. If you enjoy coding, I suggest that you check out Project Euler. I have solved a few of the easier problems and have added the solutions to this repository. In the 'Blog' here, I plan to write about the places I have travelled to, file recipes of dishes that I cooked and liked, and gab about things happening around me.
