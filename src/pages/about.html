about me

Currently, I am working in the Centre for Science Communication and Outreach at IMSc Chennai. (If you are at IMSc and want to see what I've been doing check this.)

I did my master's in Physics with a minor in Mathematics at the National Institute of Science Education and Research (NISER) Bhubaneswar. My master's thesis was on Deuteron Production in Relativistic Heavy Ion Collision.

During the covid-19 pandemic, I taught Physics and Mathematics to high school and first-year engineering students online. Teaching got me interested in the pedagogical research on mathematics and science education.

I visited the Tata Institute of Fundamental Research (TIFR) Mumbai in 2018 and the Inter-University Accelerator Centre (IUAC) New Delhi in the summer of 2016.

In college, I was the student-in-charge of the nature club, OorNa, and the literary club, LitC.
