about me

I'm currently working at the Centre for Science Communication and Outreach at IMSc Chennai. (If you're at IMSc, feel free to check out what I've been up to by clicking here).

I completed my master's in Physics with a minor in Mathematics at the National Institute of Science Education and Research (NISER) in Bhubaneswar. My master's thesis was on Deuteron Production in Relativistic Heavy Ion Collision.

During the Covid-19 pandemic, I taught Physics and Mathematics online to high school and first-year engineering students. This experience got me interested in the pedagogical research on mathematics and science education.

In the past, I had the opportunity to visit the Tata Institute of Fundamental Research (TIFR) in Mumbai in 2018 and the Inter-University Accelerator Centre (IUAC) in New Delhi in the summer of 2016.

Back in college, I served in various leadership roles such as the student-in-charge of the nature club, OorNa, and the literary club, LitC.
